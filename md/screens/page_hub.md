# Page Hub Management (`/dashboard/page/[id]`)

A specialized command center for individual aesthetic spaces, allowing granular control over items, posts, and settings.

## Tabs & Navigation

### 1. Items Management (`items` tab)
- **Inventory Control**: Tabular view of all artifacts within the space.
- **Actions**: Add, Edit, and Delete items.
- **Visuals**: Displays thumbnails, pricing, and tags.

### 2. Showcase Feed (`feed` tab)
- **Content Management**: List of posts associated with the space.
- **Engagement Insights**: Displays Likes and Comments per post.
- **Visibility Control**: Status indicators for Public/Private manifests.

### 3. Space Settings (`settings` tab)
- **Identity Config**: Update page name and meta-data.
- **Aesthetic Display**: Read-only visualization of the current space theme and custom tokens.

## Navigation Context
- **View Public**: Direct link to the creator's live domain (`/user/[slug]`).
- **Dashboard Return**: Quick navigation back to the Creator Studio overview.
